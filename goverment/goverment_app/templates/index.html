Why Government released New Schemes in India ??

The government releases new schemes in India for a variety of reasons. To promote development: The government may introduce new schemes to promote the development of a particular region or sector. To support specific groups: The government may release new schemes to support specific groups such as farmers, women, and minorities. To encourage investment: The government may introduce new schemes to encourage investment in certain sectors, such as infrastructure or renewable energy. To fulfill election promises: The government may introduce new schemes to fulfill election promises made to the electorate. Overall, the government releases new schemes in India to improve the lives of its citizens, promote development, and achieve its policy goals.
